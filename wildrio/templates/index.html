About Us

Welcome to Wild Rio

Kakkadanpoil is a hilly village bordering Wayanad, Kozhikode and Malappuram Wild Rio Campsite is located at an altitude of about 3000 feet above sea level The year-round waterfall, cool water and warmth is an experience that cannot be found anywhere else The sight of "Pantheeraayiram" forests spread on both sides will thrill both the eyes and the mind. Famous for its variety of upland crops like coffee, caste, and cocoa, Thottapalli is a dreamland that welcomes a nature lover with grasslands, streams, rare birds, and butterflies. A breath-taking jeep ride over rocks on adventurous off-road routes Home stay, tent stay, view points and camp fire spots are available in Wild Rio for travelers. "This place is heaven" in one word.
